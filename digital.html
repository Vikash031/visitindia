Digital India & UPI for Foreigners

Embrace the digital revolution in India

Digital India

Digital India is a campaign launched by the Government of India to ensure the country's services are made available to citizens electronically by improving online infrastructure and increasing Internet connectivity.

It aims to empower citizens with digital literacy and provide access to digital services.

UPI Use for Foreigners

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is a real-time payment system developed by the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI). It allows instant transfer of money between bank accounts using a mobile device.

Foreign tourists can now use the UPI One World app to make seamless digital payments across India without needing an Indian mobile number[^1^][1][^2^][2].

How to Use UPI One World

  • Download the UPI One World app from the App Store or Play Store.
  • Verify your documents and complete KYC at the issuer counter at airports or other locations in India.
  • Load cash into the wallet using foreign currency or credit/debit cards.
  • Use the app to scan QR codes and make payments at various merchant locations.
  • Encash any remaining balance before leaving the country[^1^][1][^2^][2].
