Visit India Rules and Regulations

Important guidelines for a safe and enjoyable visit

General Rules

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Always carry a valid ID.
  • Follow traffic rules and regulations.
  • Dispose of waste properly and keep the environment clean.
  • Avoid public displays of affection.

Specific Regulations

Ensure you have the appropriate visa for your stay. Follow all immigration guidelines and keep your documents handy.
Stay updated on health advisories. Carry necessary medications and follow safety protocols to ensure your well-being.
